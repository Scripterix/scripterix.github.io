About Me

Piotr Adamkowski, aka Scripterix

Web Developer & E-commerce Specialist since 2008

My web development journey started in 2008 when I needed to create an online store but couldn't find a solid programmer within my budget. I thought, "If others can create online stores, I can learn this too." That's how my internet programming adventure began.

Since 2008

17+ Years of Web Development

The Beginning

After 1.5 months of research, server and domain purchase, I installed my first store, Sweetshop.pl, using Zend Commerce. Sweetshop specialized in ready-made products and materials for decoupage. The store operated successfully and expanded its reach, with sales enabling further development.

Magento Era

In early 2008, Magento appeared as e-commerce software. I migrated my store from Zend Commerce to Magento 1.09. The new store looked and performed better, offering more features. SEO optimization was excellent, with meta tag options on pages, products, and categories.

During this time, I had to learn various technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP - all essential for Magento development. I also learned how the Zend Framework works and created most graphic projects using Photoshop.
